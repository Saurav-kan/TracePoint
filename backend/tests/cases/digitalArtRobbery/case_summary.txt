Case Summary: The Midnight Glitch

On March 4th, at approximately 11:45 PM, the "Neo-Vanguard" Digital Art Gallery experienced a catastrophic system breach. The gallery's centerpiece, a unique generative NFT masterpiece titled "The Infinite Pulse," valued at over $2.5 million, was replaced by a flickering static image.

Preliminary findings:
- System breach detected at 23:45.
- Main art server "AETHER-01" logs show unauthorized root access using 'aris_admin' credentials.
- Security cameras in the server room experienced a 30-second loop.
- A physical security token was missing from the curator's office.

Relevant People:
- Sarah Vane: Lead IT Engineer. Responsible for the gallery's digital infrastructure and blockchain security.
- Dr. Aris: Head Curator. Oversees the art collection and holds high-level administrative access.
- Marcus Thorne: Security Guard on duty. Responsible for physical patrols and monitoring surveillance feeds.
- Officer J. Miller: Lead Forensic Investigator from the Cyber-Crimes Division.
