Interview Transcript: Officer J. Miller and Security Guard Marcus Thorne
Date: March 5th, 02:30 AM
Location: Neo-Vanguard Gallery Lobby

Miller: Marcus, walk me through what happened at 11:45.

Thorne: It was a normal shift, Officer. I was doing my rounds in the East Wing. Everything looked quiet. I checked the server room door at 11:30, and it was locked tight.

Miller: Did you notice anything unusual? Any weird sounds or flickering lights?

Thorne: Now that you mention it, around 11:40, my tablet—the one we use for surveillance—lagged for a few seconds. I thought it was just the Wi-Fi. Then, at 11:47, the alarm went off.

Miller: Did you see anyone?

Thorne: Nobody. But when I got to the server room after the alarm, the door was slightly ajar. Not forced, just... open. Like someone had a key or a card.

Miller: Who has access to that room?

Thorne: Just the Head Curator, Dr. Aris, and the Lead IT Engineer, Sarah Vane. And me, but only for emergencies.
