On the night of February 28th, 2026, the "Aegis" encryption keys were stolen from a high-security vault. The initial investigation points exclusively to Elena Vance because her badge was used for entry and her laptop was the device that executed the theft. However, Vance claims she was on a different floor fixing an espresso machine. The investigation needs to resolve the contradiction between the digital evidence (the "who") and the environmental/physical evidence (the "how" and "where").
