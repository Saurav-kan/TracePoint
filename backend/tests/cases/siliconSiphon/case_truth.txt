The ground truth of this case is that Director Elena Vance is innocent and was framed by Security Lead Marcus Thorne. Thorne used a "Replay Attack" to clone Vance’s physical badge and planted her personal laptop in the vault to create a "perfect" digital and physical trail.

The critical flaw in his plan was the Elevator Load Sensor. While Thorne could spoof Vance's digital identity, he could not spoof his own physical weight. The sensors recorded a 180 lb occupant in the elevator leading to the vault, which matches Thorne’s physical profile and is physically impossible for the 125 lb Director Vance.

Timeline:
- 10:05 PM: Marcus Thorne enters elevator in Building A.
- 10:12 PM: Thorne (using Vance's badge) enters the High-Security Vault.
- 10:15 PM: "Aegis" encryption keys are copied to a USB drive.
- 10:18 PM: Thorne leaves the vault.
- 10:20 PM: Vance's laptop is left in the server rack.
- 10:25 PM: Thorne returns to his patrol.

Evidence:
- elevator_maintenance_log.txt: Shows 180 lbs at 10:05 PM.
- employee_hr_directory.md: Shows Thorne (180 lbs) and Vance (125 lbs).
- espresso_machine_status.txt: Confirms Vance's alibi (fixing machine at 10:10 PM).
- wifi_connection_history.csv: Shows Vance's phone in the breakroom at 10:12 PM.
